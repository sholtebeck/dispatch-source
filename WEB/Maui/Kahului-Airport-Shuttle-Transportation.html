Most Affordable Door-to-Door Shared Ride Transportation

Hawaii Executive Transportation offers a door-to-door shared ride airport shuttle transportation to West and South Maui including the towns of Kaanapali, Kapalua, Kihei, Wailea, Lahaina, Makena, Honokowai, Napili and all parts in between.  Our airport shuttle can handle special baggage such as surfboards, windsurf boards, stand up paddle boards, bicycles et al...  For an additional fee we can also greet you with a flower lei which is the traditional and perfect way to start your Maui vacation.  

Exclusive Airport Transfers and Private Charters 

For those seeking the ultimate in luxury travel we also offer an array of vehicles for exclusive transportation to your hotel or where ever your ultimate destination may be.  Our fleet of vehicles include limousines, Mercedes sprinter vans, sport utility vehicles and town cars.  Our experienced and friendly staff will greet you by the escalator and will assist you from there.  There is no better way to arrive in paradise than by being privately chauffeured in our private cars.  We can also accommodate larger parties with our 14 and  25 passenger mini-buses.


Arrival/Departure Instructions

Shared Shuttle Service

Upon your arrival if you do not see a greeter with a sign that says your name please give us a call on the courtesy phone located in baggage claim area dial 55 and we will send you out to your pick up location. We do not guarantee greets for arrivals it is upon availability ONLY. 

Be advised delays may occur as this service is shared with other passengers and will be required to make other stops. You will be scheduled to ride with passengers that arrive within 15 minutes of your originally scheduled arrival time if your flight is early or delayed you will be placed on the first available shuttle.

For late night shuttle arrivals be advised our service will accommodate you as long as your flight lands before 11:30 PM if your flight is delayed and arrives anytime after 11:30 PM your run will AUTO CANCEL and you will need to find other means of transportation. You will not be penalized for this type of cancellation and any monies prepaid will be refunded in full.

Exclusive Service

(Town-Car/ Limousine/ Taxi/ Exclusive Van / Mini-Bus)

Upon your arrival you will need to look for a greeter with your name on a sign. The greeter will meet you in the baggage claim area or at the bottom of the escalator. Greeters do not handle luggage, however they can help you obtain porter service at a rate of $3 per bag handled. If you do not make contact with your greeter, please feel free to call us on any courtesy phone in the baggage claim area by dialing 55 or call 808-669-2300.

Departure Instructions

Please meet your driver at the valet desk of hotel, or main office of your condo. . Be sure to advise dispatch of any special instructions prior to transfer date.

CARSEAT LAW

Although we are exempt from the State of Hawaii Car seat law, car seat rentals are available  to you at $7 per seat. We suggest that you bring your own child seat but can provide one for you upon request and availability. Please also keep in mind installation and inspection of the car seat is the sole responsibility of the parent or guardian traveling with the minor.
