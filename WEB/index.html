Hawaii Airport Shuttle and Transportation.


Proudly serving Hawaii since 1990

Hawaii Executive Transportation -- For over 27 years, Hawaii Executive Transportation Executive Airport Shuttle has been providing the most dependable and affordable transportation on the island of Maui, including a Kahului airport shuttle and a Kapalua airport shuttle service. For your convenience we provide an online reservation system for you to book your transportation to and from the airport.

We also provide the ultimate in luxurious private car services. Our fleet of vehicles include limousines, sedans, and sports utility vehicles. Perfect for airport transportation, weddings, corporate events, night out on the town and much more. Take the stress out of driving and enjoy paradise!

For larger groups we also offer mini-bus charter services. Our fleet of charter vehicles include 14 and 25 passenger mini-buses and Mercedes Sprinter Vans. Perfect for Island tours, airport transportation and corporate events. Our friendly and professional staff will get your group to the event safely and affordably.
