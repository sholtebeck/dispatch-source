Hawaii's #1 Transportation Choice Since 1990

Professional, reliable service!

Established in 1990 as a one-vehicle company, Hawaii Executive Transportation Service has since grown to over 50 vehicles including limousines, sedans, shuttles, taxis, minibuses, and wheelchair accessible vehicles.

Hawaii Executive Transportation Service is now operated by Speedi Shuttle Hawaii, the leading provider of ground transportation shuttle services in the state of Hawaii.

Hawaii Executive Transportation Service is contracted by most of the major hotels and resorts in Ka'anapali including the Hyatt, Westin Maui, and Sheraton Maui.
