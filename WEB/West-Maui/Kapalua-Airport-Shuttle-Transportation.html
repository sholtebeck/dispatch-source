Kapalua Airport Shuttle to Ka'anapali, Kapalua and more

Most Affordable Door-to-Door Shared Ride Transportation

Hawaii Executive Transportation offers a door-to-door shared ride airport shuttle transportation from the West Maui airport to homes and resorts in the surrounding area including Ka'anapali, Kapalua, Lahaina, Honokowai, Kahana and Napili. Our airport shuttle can handle special baggage such as surfboards, windsurf boards, stand up paddle boards, bicycles et al... For an additional fee we can also greet you with a flower lei which is the traditional and perfect way to start your Maui vacation.

Exclusive Airport Transfers and Private Charters

For those seeking the ultimate in luxury travel we also offer an array of vehicles for exclusive transportation to your final destination. Our fleet of luxury vehicles include limousines, Mercedes sprinter vans, sport utility vehicles and town cars. Our experienced and friendly staff will greet you by the escalator and will assist you from there. There is no better way to arrive in paradise than by being privately chauffeured in our private cars. We can also accommodate larger parties with our 14 and 25 passenger mini-buses.
